1. Platform Architecture & Layers

🛤️

Process Intelligence Layer (The Orchestrator)

Acts as the production line rails. It enforces business rules, monitors workflows in real time, tracks process stages, and handles automated process execution triggered by client actions.

🧬

Data & Relationship Layer (BHub Graph)

A centralized knowledge base mapping relationships between customers, suppliers, compliance entities, and regulations. It provides deep context to AI models, ensuring consistent cross-system decisions.

🤖

Automation Layer (Agentic AI)

Autonomous decision-making layer that executes tasks based on process rules and BHub Graph context. Powers conversational AI (BSamurai) and autonomous ledger operations.
